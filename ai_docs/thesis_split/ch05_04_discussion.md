## **5.4 Discussion**

Based on the experimental results, it can be concluded that the proposed **ELM– PSO model** significantly outperforms the conventional ELM approach for shortterm electric load forecasting. The optimization of hidden-layer parameters using PSO not only enhances prediction accuracy but also ensures consistent performance across repeated experiments.

The strong convergence behavior, accurate load tracking, and improved statistical stability validate the effectiveness of the proposed hybrid approach and confirm its suitability for practical short-term load forecasting applications.
